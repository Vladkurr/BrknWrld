History
  • Our story begins back in 2055, when everything was still fixable. But there were more and more people, less and less food, and the air was getting dirtier. So by 2063, Wilforth Donegan built space arks that could accommodate up to 1,000 people and could deliver people to Mars in two years. Such ships were built for another five years, but by 2068 it was already impossible to be on earth. The plants rotted, almost all the animals died out, and with them the people who did not have time to wait for their ark.
  • So in 2069, there were already 278 ships in orbit that started moving to Mars. But 13 ships were not completed. They had no engines, they were supposed to be assembled on earth and delivered to the arks, but there were no survivors left on earth. These ships were suitable for life in orbit, but not for traveling between planets. So 13 unfortunate ships remained in orbit for another 48 years.
  • My name is Ben Miller and my parents were on the ark N*273 which could not fly away. According to their stories, life on earth was... well, at least it was life. I was born already on the ship in 2107. There were not many children, as most did not want their children to grow up on the ship, they wanted the best. But life on the ship was not bad, we went to school, we didn't have classes, we studied engineering, learned how to print food on a 3D printer. Studied chemistry. I really liked to collect something with my hands. Bobi Brown, the chief engineer of the ship and part-time our teacher taught us a lot, without him we probably would not have been alive…
  • But I won't get ahead of myself. In 2117, Bobi and his team decided to try to go down to earth, but there were not enough boats, and then our captain made a list of people who do not have hostile diseases and who can have children. And unfortunately my only friend Eddie couldn't fly, he had diabetes and was told that he had 10 years left at most. It's not fair. But I do not lose hope, because Bobi stayed with those who could not fly away and I am sure that he will help them.
  • So we ended up on earth, it was completely different, not like in the stories of my parents. It was too easy to breathe on it. Grass and thickets were everywhere. As soon as we landed we saw a flock of birds, it was very cool. But we haven't seen anyone else yet. We've been on earth for a week now. We found a small village while we live here, but the adults say it's better not to stay here for a long time…
